Case study
Beach Front Villas

Background

Beach Front Villas are a new start up business who provide luxury villa rentals on the stunning Turks & Caicos Islands in the Caribbean. Robin Spruce, Beach Front Villas owner and founder approached us to produce a class leading, visually stunning and easy to use website. The site we designed and built a site puts the fantastic islands and amazing luxury villas to the fore and firmly estamblshes Beach Front Villas as leaders within their market.

Design

When you're apealing to a luxury, high end market that can afford to spend upto $20,000 USD per night on a villa in the Caribbean, it's important that your web site looks and feels just as high end and luxurious. We had a clean sheet of papper with which to approach the design and through a mix of classic & contemtory typography, a fresh, vibrant colour pallet and georgous photography we delivered a site looks just as stunning as its subject matter. Couple that with the user experance and information architecture work we and you end up with a site thats easy to use and navigate and looks stunning. Form and function in perfect harmony.

Technology

Beach Front Villas is based on the excellent Roots Bedrock framework. This gives us a flexible development environment in addition to seperation of Wordpress core files and our custom theme for production. Use of Composer allows us to control plugin and Wordpress versions easily. The site had a requirement to be able to manage bookings for each villa and present available villas to a user based on a start and end date. We wrote a custom search plugin to grab available villas based on a custom booking post type attached to each villa. These results were then presented to the user so they could see exactly which villas were available on the dates provided.

"The guys form Flo have been brilliant! Great communication throughout the project and they nailed the site, both from a design and functionality point of view. A pleasure to work with."
Robin Spruce - Beach Front Villas
