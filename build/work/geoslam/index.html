Case study
GeoSLAM

Background

GeoSLAM are a Nottingham-based technology start-up who develop survey solutions for the measurement and mapping of multi-level three-dimensional environments. The new bespoke-built WordPress site not only looks fantastic but is also easy to navigate, update and is optimised for mobile and desktop devices.

Design

Taking our lead from GeoSLAM's vibrant orange and black branding we produced a striking design that incorporated some of the fantastic 3D point cloud imagery generated from their scanning products. The design is responsive and optimised to work across desktop, tablet and mobile devices so users get a great experience no matter how they access the site.

Technology

GeoSLAM wanted to be able to make minor text edits to the site as well as add content such as new staff members, image & video galleries, case studies and news stories. WordPress was the ideal platform to use to provide this functionality. We produced a bespoke-built theme to maximise the speed of the site and customised the back-end interface to make updates to the site a doddle. We made use of Roots' excellent Bedrock framework, allowing us to use Composer to control plugin upgrades as well as have a modern, best-practice development environment.
